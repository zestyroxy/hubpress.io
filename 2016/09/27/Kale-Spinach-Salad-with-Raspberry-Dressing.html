Kale & Spinach Salad with Raspberry Dressing

27 September 2016 on [vegan, salad, kale, spinach, raspberry]

It’s dreary outside right now and we all need a boost of vitamin and a bit of freshness in our bowls. That’s why I decided to make this bright salad today.

Raspberry Dressing

Dressing Ingredients

  • 15 to 20 fresh or frozen raspberries

  • 5 ml red wine vinegar

  • 5 ml olive oil

  • 5 ml maple syrup (add more if you have a sweet tooth)

  • 5 ml water (add more for a thinner dressing)

  • 5 gm chia seeds or poppy seeds

Instructions

  1. To start, you will want to thaw your raspberries about an hour in advance if you will be using frozen raspberries.

  2. Put all the ingredients in a blender or food processor and mix until the consitency is smoothe.

Note: This makes a serving for one person if the meal will consist of a salad or two people if the salad is going to be a side dish.

Kale & Spinach Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 large kale leaves

  • 2 handfuls of spinach

  • 20 g pine nuts

  • 1 spring onion

  • 1/3 lemon

  • 1 apple (I used Macintosh because they are in season right now in Canada)

  • 3 ml coconut oil

Instructions

  1. Remove the hard stem portion of the kale and cut the leaves in very thin slices with a knife.

  2. Put the kale in a medium sized bowl and squeeze 1/3 of a lemon’s juice over it. Massage the juice into the kale with your hands. This will soften the kale.

  3. In the meantime, heat up 3 ml of coconut oil in a pan over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the pine nuts and roast them until golden. This should only take a few minutes.

  4. Cut your apple into thin slices and your spring onion into small rings.

  5. Add the spinach over the kale and mix so the spinach is evenly distributed.

  6. Add the apple slices, spring onion rings, roasted pine nuts and raspberry dressing.

  7. Add other ingredients that you fancy, like avocado!

Your salad is ready to be devoured…​ daintily!
